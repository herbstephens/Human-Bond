# HumanBond Brand

Visual identity for HumanBond.

## Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Primary (dark) | **Indigo Night** | `#0F0F23` |
| Primary (midtone) | **Twilight** | `#1A1D3A` |
| Accent | **Bond Coral** | `#E63946` |
| Accent (deep) | **Bond Claret** | `#B02A37` |
| Highlight | **TIME Gold** | `#F1B24A` |
| Neutral light | **Bone** | `#FDFDFB` |
| Neutral muted | **Slate** | `#6C6F93` |

**Usage ratio:** 60–70% Indigo Night (primary), 15–20% Bone (surfaces & text contrast), 10–15% Bond Coral (accents & CTAs), occasional TIME Gold for TIME-related callouts.

## Typography

- **Headings:** Georgia (serif, warmth for an intimate subject matter)
- **Body:** Calibri / system-ui (clean, readable)
- **Mono (addresses, code):** Consolas / ui-monospace

## Logo

Two interlocking rings — one indigo, one coral — with a gold dot at the point of intersection.

- The rings represent two verified humans
- The intersection represents the bond: the shared treasury, the jointly-held TIME, the cryptographic commitment
- The gold dot is the TIME yield accruing at the point of union
